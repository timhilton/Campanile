# Privacy Policy

Campanile does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 
The app runs entirely on your device and has no network connectivity. 
No analytics, no tracking, no data of any kind leaves your Mac.

All preferences (volume, quiet hours, mute state) are stored locally 
on your device using macOS standard storage and are never shared.
